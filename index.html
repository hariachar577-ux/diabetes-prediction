About The Model

How it works

Inputs

The model uses eight common health indicators often seen in diabetes screening datasets.

Training

A logistic regression model is trained in JavaScript with normalized features and gradient descent.

Output

The score is a probability estimate, grouped into low, moderate, or high risk bands for readability.
